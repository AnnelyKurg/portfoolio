Hi, nice to meet you!

I’m Annely and I have a dream of becoming a web developer.


This fall I started the journey to make this dream a reality and went to study web development in Tartu Vocational College – the culmination of a long time spent self-analyzing and trying to figure out who I want to be when I grow up.


I have been working in different positions in sales throughout my adult life. For the last couple of years, I have become involved in various IT projects, while working as an account manager. Today I am responsible for overseeing the sales process implementation in our custom IT solution, developed in-house. Testing, making bug reports and suggestions for improvements is part of my daily work. Doing all that, I have found myself thinking that I do not want to do sales as a full-time job anymore! Instead, I want to be in the IT field.


After realizing that, I had to find out what exactly makes my eyes sparkle in the humongous IT-world. Like every person in today’s world – I googled… a lot. This is how I found the Web Development curriculum in Tartu Vocational College. After taking a course in Codecademy.com about HTML and CSS, my eyes sparkled and here I am! Optimistic that I can be a good student by mastering the craft of coding while continuing to be a reliable and responsible colleague at work and having time for my family… for the next 2,5 years (sigh).
